including small language-model training runs, translation experiments, and
investigations into alternative decoding or acceleration methods.

Specific threads have included screenplay language modeling, French–English
translation on Europarl, and experiments inspired by LLaDA / LoSA-style
inference. These are exploratory projects aimed at building intuition rather
than producing polished systems.
