Altair is a UCI chess engine I built to explore search, evaluation, and
performance engineering. It uses traditional chess-engine techniques such as
alpha-beta search, move ordering, and optimized evaluation, and it became one of
my first substantial systems-style projects.

The engine reached superhuman strength and has received thousands of downloads.
Working on it taught me a lot about profiling, low-level optimization, and the
practical tradeoffs between search depth, evaluation quality, and raw speed.
