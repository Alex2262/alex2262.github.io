Building and training small transformer language models from scratch to
understand attention, optimization dynamics, and the practical details that
don't show up in the math: tokenization choices, learning-rate schedules, and
how training behaves on limited data.

The goal here is depth of understanding rather than scale — small enough to
train and iterate on quickly, but complete enough to see how the pieces fit
together.
